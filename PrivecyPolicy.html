Privacy Policy (Including User Data Deletion Policy)

Last Updated: 09-08-2025

Beyond Collective Private Limited (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Profile details (e.g., Instagram/YouTube usernames)
  • Demographic information

b. Usage Information

  • Device data, IP address, browser type
  • Pages visited, time spent, clickstream data

c. Third-Party Data

  • Information from connected platforms like Google, Meta, YouTube, etc., if you choose to connect them

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Personalize content and user experience
  • Communicate with you (notifications, updates, support)
  • Ensure platform safety and prevent fraud
  • Analyze usage trends and improve features

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data with:

  • Trusted third-party service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, payment processors)
  • Legal authorities if required by law
  • Partners or brands only with your explicit consent

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve functionality
  • Understand usage patterns
  • Serve personalized content or offers

You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Storage and Security

We store data on secure servers with encryption and access control. However, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your password confidential.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccuracies
  • Request data deletion (see below)
  • Withdraw consent

You can request these by emailing us at Contact@gobeyond.now.

7. User Data Deletion Policy

You have full control over your data. Deletion can be done by:

  • Using your profile/account settings (if applicable)
  • Contacting us at Contact@gobeyond.now with the subject line: “Data Deletion Request”

Upon request, we will delete:

  • Your personal information
  • Connected third-party tokens
  • Account-related activity (except anonymized analytics)

Timeline: All requests will be processed within 7 business days.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as needed for providing services, legal compliance, and resolving disputes. Inactive accounts may be deleted after 24 months of inactivity.

10. International Data Transfers

If you are accessing our services outside India, you consent to your information being transferred to and stored in India or other jurisdictions where we or our partners operate.

11. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may revise this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification.
